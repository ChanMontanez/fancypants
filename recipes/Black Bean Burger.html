Vegetarian Black Bean Burger

Black Bean Burgers

  • 16oz can black beans drained
  • 16 can lentils, drained
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup crushed tortilla chips
  • 1/2 medium onion, finely diced
  • 1 to 2 jalapenos diced (If that sounds too spicy, you can use half of a bell pepper instead)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
